Principal Investigator

Dr. Chao Fan

Dr. Chao Fan leads an interdisciplinary research group at Clemson University focused on AI agents and cyber-physical systems. The lab develops data-driven and human-centered methods for high-impact application domains including emergency management, transportation, urban systems, and risk analysis.

Our lab is always recruiting prospective and self-motivated students to work with us. We welcome students from a wide range of backgrounds, including computer science, civil engineering, GIS, economics, remote sensing, electrical engineering, industrial engineering, operations research, information systems, control, and automation.

Research Directions

Where computation meets the physical world.

AI for Emergency Operations

Agentic AI models and solutions for situational awareness, emergency dispatch, resource allocation, planning, response, and recovery.

Transportation Digital Twins

Digital twin methods for modeling mobility systems, road networks, autonomous vehicles, safety, accessibility, and emerging transportation technologies.

Cyber-Physical Systems

Architecture, design, sensing, and security for smart connected communities and societal-scale cyber-physical systems.
